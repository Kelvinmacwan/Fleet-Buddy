About Fleet Buddy

Your trusted partner for complete fleet operations

Fleet Buddy is a professional fleet management and transport support company helping truck owners, fleet operators, logistics companies, and transport businesses across India streamline operations, reduce costs, maintain compliance, and maximize vehicle uptime — all from a single point of contact.

Running a fleet is about more than just trucks — it's about keeping your business profitable, compliant, and moving without interruptions. Our goal is simple: help fleet owners reduce costs, save valuable time, and make smarter business decisions — so you can focus on growing your fleet while we take care of the rest.
